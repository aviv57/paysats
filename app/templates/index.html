Welcome to Paysats Online

Paysats Online is a project focused on creating a unified, email-like identity for Bitcoin users.

Instead of juggling multiple handles and addresses across platforms, Paysats provides a single memorable address like yourname@paysats.online that resolves to:

  • Contact Information: Nickname, Twitter/X handle, email, and Nostr public key
  • Bitcoin Payment Options: On-chain address, Lightning Address, Silent Payments, and more

This system is designed to help wallets, social platforms, and users interact more easily with decentralized identity and payments.
