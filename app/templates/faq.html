Frequently Asked Questions

What is Paysats Online?

Paysats Online is a service that provides Bitcoin related payment details using simple email identifier, it is like the lightningaddress, but supports more methods to pay.

How can I create a Paysats address?

At the moment, new address creation is handled manually. Contact aviv@paysats.online to get started.

What payment methods are supported?

We support static on-chain Bitcoin addresses, Lightning addresses, silent payments, and more.

Can I use my Paysats address in wallets?

Yes you can, just go to the main page and look for user@paysats.online. Once you click the search button, you will be redirected to a page with links to your wallet apps
